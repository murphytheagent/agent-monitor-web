Why it belongs here

Three reasons this is worth browsing

The gallery is for project surfaces that make an idea clearer by being interactive. The tokenizer page earns that spot because it turns a fuzzy model-comparison question into something you can inspect immediately.

It answers a real model-selection question

If two checkpoints share the same tokenizer, the vocabulary is not the variable. The workbench makes that visible instead of leaving it as repo folklore.

It surfaces prompt-format differences

Chat templates and special tokens often matter as much as vocabulary boundaries. The tool lets you inspect those formatting choices directly.

It invites useful poking around

This is the kind of page where you can paste a sentence, switch families, and immediately learn something from the result. That is the bar for the rest of the gallery too.
